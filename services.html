Our Services

As society becomes more reliant on technology, cyber security has become a critical concern for businesses and individuals alike. Cyber threats are becoming more sophisticated and frequent, making it essential to have a robust cyber security strategy in place.

We offer a range of services, including Cyber Risk Assurance, Governance, Risk & Compliance, and Cyber Defence Services. Our Cyber Risk Assurance service helps businesses identify and prepare for potential cyber attacks. We conduct simulated attacks to test your security program and evaluate your team's readiness to detect and respond to these attacks.

Our Governance, Risk & Compliance service helps organizations align their IT and technological innovation with business objectives while managing risk and meeting industry and government regulations. We develop comprehensive security policies and guidelines to address your organization's risk and compliance requirements..

Our Cyber Defence Services help businesses improve their defenses against attacks by assessing their current capabilities and identifying security gaps. We design, build, and operate a 24/7 Managed Security Operations Center (SOC) to monitor and respond to potential threats.We understand that cyber security is not a one-time problem. It requires continuous monitoring and improvement to stay ahead of evolving cyber threats.

Our cyber security offering is designed to keep your business secure and running smoothly wherever your team may be. Liquid Intelligent Technologies will provide you with ongoing management, monitoring and support to help protect your business from malicious cyber attacks.
